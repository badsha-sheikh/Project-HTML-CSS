My Traveling Blog
Jaflong, Sylhet

A perfect blend of green hills, flowing rivers, and tribal life. As you arrive, the view of Dawki River with transparent water feels surreal. You’ll see stone collectors working knee-deep in water, and Khasi tribal villages up in the hills.

Khoiyachora Hills, Sitakundu

A thrilling mix of hills and waterfalls. After a steep hike through forested trails, you’ll reach Khoiyachora or Sohosrodhara Falls, feeling like a mini adventure. Peaceful views from Chandranath Temple, perched high above the plain, are rewarding.

Laboni Beach, Cox's Bazar

Standing at the edge of the world’s longest natural sea beach, you feel the vastness of the Bay of Bengal stretch beyond the horizon. The roar of waves, golden sunsets, and beachside life create a unique coastal vibe.

Tri-Mohona, Chandpur

A laid-back riverside town famous for hilsha (ilish) fish. The Padma–Meghna river confluence is powerful to watch. In the afternoon, take a launch ride — you’ll feel the wind, hear the water crash, and see golden sunsets.
